Project 1: Data Analytics
The dataset for this project involved survey data mainly from the US on individuals' titles, roles, demographics, and salary. I was tasked with coming up with marketing suggestions for where to target advertising for a fake company that matches employees to positions, and then makes money based on that employee's salary.
