Project 2: Database Structures
The three databases we worked on for this group project were MySQL, Neo4j, and MongoDB. We created the data for the databases based on ourselves in order to examine the differences between these three systems. After inputting all of our data and practicing some queries on each database, we wrote a Medium article for database novices, which you can find below. We also compared the three databases from a more technical standpoint and their pros and cons in a presentation, which is also below.
